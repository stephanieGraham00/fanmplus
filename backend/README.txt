♥ FANM+ BACKEND SERVER ♥
===========================

Sa ou bezwen:
1. Node.js (telechaje sou https://nodejs.org)
2. WiFi (pou lòt moun konekte sou menm rezo)

--- KOMANSE ---

1. Enstale Node.js: ale sou https://nodejs.org, telechaje LTS, enstale
2. Ouvri dosye FanmPlus-Backend
3. Double-klik sou "start.bat"
4. Premye fwa: lap enstale pakè yo (tann 1-2 minit)
5. Lè w wè "Mwen pare pou sèvi tout fanm! 🌸" nan fenèt la, serveur a pare

--- KOTE OUVE ---

Sou òdinatè a:   http://localhost:3000
Sou telefòn (menm WiFi): http://[IP-ADRES]:3000
Admin:           http://localhost:3000/admin

--- Jwenn IP Adres ou ---

Louve yon fenèt PowerShell, tape: ipconfig
Chache "IPv4 Address" — egzanp: 192.168.1.5
Lè sa a, lòt moun ka ale sou: http://192.168.1.5:3000

--- KISA BACKEND LA FÈ ---

- Tout itilizatè yo nan baz done santral (fanmplus.db)
- Ou ka wè moun k ap konekte nan admin la
- Login/Register ap mache atravè rezo a
- Randevou, jounal, posts tout nan baz done

--- ADMIN ---

Ale sou: http://localhost:3000/admin
Imèl: admin@fanmplus.com
Modpas: admin123

--- OFFLINE VS ONLINE ---

Nan ekran koneksyon an, gen yon checkbox "Mode serveur (backend)"
- Koche = itilize baz done santral (tout moun wè menm bagay)
- Dekoche = itilize localStorage (chak moun sou pwòp aparèy yo)

--- BLAGE NÒT ---

Si ou vle tout moun nan mond lan ka itilize app la (pa sèlman WiFi),
fòk ou deplwaye sou entènèt (Heroku, Render, Railway).
M ap ede w fè sa si ou vle.
